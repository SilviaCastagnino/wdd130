Italy

Italy is a country located in Southern Europe, known for its rich history, culture, cuisine and artistic heritage. It is characterized by its boot shape and borders France, Switzerland, Austria and Slovenia. It is bathed by the Mediterranean Sea, which includes the Adriatic Sea, the Ionian Sea and the Tyrrhenian Sea. Its capital is Rome, rich in history and monuments.
